About me

I’ve always been drawn to the stories that shape our culture, whether through music, film, or fashion. Over the years, I’ve built a career breaking down the sounds and visuals that move us, always looking beyond the surface to uncover what really matters.

As the CEO of CTDA Media, I lead a team committed to Connecting the Dots Among Blacks, telling our stories with depth, honesty, and insight. Through the CTDA Media Podcast, I get to have real, unfiltered conversations about the hits, the misses, and the moments that define our industry.

Music has taken me places I never imagined. From the front lines of pop culture to the Grammy Awards, I’ve had the privilege of witnessing the magic of music’s biggest night. But my work goes beyond criticism. I also help artists refine their teams, shape their vision, and cut through the noise to build a brand that lasts.

Fashion and art have always been part of my world. Most people don’t know it runs in my family, but that love led me to create CTDA Fashion, a brand that’s set to return with fresh designs that reflect both culture and creativity.

At the heart of everything I do, I want to spark conversations, challenge perspectives, and celebrate the art that brings us together. So whether you’re here to check out my latest review, dive into a debate, or collaborate on something meaningful, let’s connect.

Humanitarian Work

Giving back has always been at the core of what I do. Over the years, I’ve supported causes that are close to my heart, particularly those that empower and uplift communities.
Why These Causes Matter: These causes matter to me because I believe in the power of community and the importance of giving people the tools to better their lives. Whether it’s through education or access to reliable energy, these initiatives are a way for me to contribute to meaningful, lasting change.

Back-to-School Donations (USA)

Every year, I donate back-to-school materials for Black children in the US to ensure they have the tools they need to succeed in their education. For me, it’s about providing opportunities and breaking down barriers. Every child deserves access to quality education, and I’m proud to help in any way I can.

Solar Panel Donations (Nigeria)

In my community in Nigeria, access to electricity has always been a challenge. To help address this, I donated solar panels to provide much-needed energy to local homes. The goal is to bring sustainable solutions to a community that has long struggled with power shortages, and I hope it inspires others to invest in long-term solutions for Africa.

Upcoming Book: Billion Dollar Dreams: From Nigeria to America

Billion Dollar Dreams: From Nigeria to America is my personal story—a journey from Nigeria to the United States, filled with challenges, triumphs, and the relentless pursuit of a dream.

This book is about more than just success; it’s about the struggles, the sacrifices, and the belief that anything is possible, no matter where you start.

This book is for anyone who needs a reminder that their dreams are valid and achievable, no matter the obstacles. It’s a story of perseverance, ambition, and the power of never giving up.
